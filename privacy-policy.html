Privacy Policy

Ramadan 2026 Calendar Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information Collection

This app does not require user registration and does not collect personal data.

Local Storage

Fasting progress and marked days are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted to any server.

Prayer Times API

Prayer times are fetched from a public API service. No personal information is sent.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications only to remind users before Fajr. No personal data is used for notifications.

Third-Party Services

This app does not share data with third parties.
